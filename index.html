SSG Joseph Dunn

Staff Sergeant Joseph Dunn is a native of Chelsea, Massachusetts. Staff Sergeant Dunn has served in the United States Army for over nine years. The United States Army has afforded Staff Sergeant Dunn the opportuinity to attend college at Full Sail University. Staff Sergeant Dunn has recently completed a masters degree in the area of game design. If you would like to learn more about how the United States Army can help you achieve your goals contact Staff Sergeant Dunn for more information.
