SFC Zachary Watkins

Sergeant First Class Zachery Watkins was born in Evansville, Indiana, and was raised in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. He graduated from Fort Lauderdale High School in June 2000. He enlisted in the United States Army in May 2000 during his senior year as an Infantryman. SFC Watkins is currently the Center Leader for the Concord, NH Army Recruiting Center. If you would like to learn more about how the United States Army can help you achieve your goals contact SFC Watkins for more information.

SFC Jarrod Heath

SFC Heath was assigned as an Army Recruiter in January of 2000.  Originally Laconia Recruiting Station and later Concord Recruiting Center upon his successful completion of the Army Recruiter Course (ARC). He has completed Airborne Training, Combat Lifesaver Course, Warrior Leaders’ Course, and Advanced Leaders’ Course (ALC) in addition to the ARC.  He also completed the Senior Leaders Course. In 1993, SFC Heath enlisted into the United States Army out of the Boston Military Entrance Processing Station (MEPS) and then shipped out a month later to Fort Benning, GA for the 11X (Infantry Recruit) School.  After a hard but enjoyable 14 weeks, PVT Heath successfully completed his training and was award the Military Occupation Specialty 11B (Infantryman). If you would like to learn more about how the United States Army can help you achieve your goals contact SFC Heath for more information.

SGT Daniel Keown

SGT Daniel W.Keown, U.S. Army Recruiter has been in this position since October 2015 as a member of the New England Recruiting Battalion, New Hampshire Company, Concord Station. SGT Keown is a graduate from Newfound Regional High School in 2007. After completion of Basic Training and Advanced Individual Training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri he was awarded the MOS 31B (Military Police Officer)  SGT Keown was first assigned to the HHC, 89th MP BDE, Fort Hood, Texas, from October to March 2012. SGT Keown deployed to Iraq from 2009 to 2010. If you would like to learn more about how the United States Army can help you achieve your goals contact SGT Keown for more information.

SSG Jeffrey Shaw

SSG Jeffery Shaw was assigned to be a United States Army Recruiter in February of 2015. He was assigned to be an Army Recruiter of the Concord Recruiting Center.  He is a native of Bangor, Maine and was born January 5, 1988.
